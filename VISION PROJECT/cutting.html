EASIEST CHICKEN BREAST RECIPE FOR MUSCLE BUILDING | FAT LOSS. (CALORIES-715Kcal,Carbs-11g,Protien-165g,Fat-13g)

STEPS TO PREPARE IT

  • Take 650 to 700 Gram Chicken And Wash it properly.
  • 2 Tbsp Curd/Dahi
  • 1 Tbsp Ginger Paste
  • 1/2 Tbsp Red Chilli Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Dhaniya Powder
  • 1 Tbsp of Salt/Namak
  • 1/2 Tbsp Black Pepper/Kaali Mirch
  • Add Them And Mix Properly
  • Marinate For 1 Hour Before Cook it
