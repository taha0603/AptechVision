EASIEST CHICKEN SANDWICH RECIPE FOR BULKING | MUSCLE BUILDING (CALORIES-1000Kcal, Carbs-12g, Protein-60g, Fat-43g)

STEPS TO PREPARE IT

  • Take 200g Chicken and wash it properly, then cook in oil.
  • Season with paprika & curry powder and cook.
  • Sauce Preparation:
  • 100g Greek Yogurt
  • 3 tbsp Mayo
  • Salt
  • Half Lemon
  • Chop coriander (dhaniya) and add to the sauce.
  • Mix all ingredients and apply on bread with lettuce.
